What we do

Promar Construction can help you with repair, maintenance, handyman services, and more for your multi-family and commercial properties. We specialize in the small repair and maintenace jobs that bigger construction companies don't typically like to work on.

Below are some examples of the type of help we can offer:

Drywall & interior repair

We perform interior repairs and patching for drywall, tiles, and other surfaces. No damage too small!

Exterior & landscape maintenace

Including lawn maintenance & mowing, snow removal, paver replacement, and more.

Painting & staining

Sanding, painting, and staining for your interior and exterior spaces. From full jobs to touch-ups.

Power washing

We'll clean up the exterior spaces of your property including driveways, sidewalks, deck areas, and more with our power washing service.

Furniture & appliances setup/repair

We hang shelves, TVs, pictures, and help assemble & repair furniture and other appliances.

Job site cleanup

Previous contractor left you with a mess? We offer sweeping and cleaning services to clean up job site messes.

Based in Golden, Colorado

Our HQ is in Golden, but we work in all surrounding areas including Boulder, Denver, and even into the mountains!

Proudly woman-owned

Promar Construction is co-owned by local Golden residents, Haley Morgan and Danielle DiLoreto, who bring complementary backgrounds to the business. Haley has a degree in civil engineering and has worked as both a civil and software engineer, and loves to problem-solve. Danielle has a degree in economics and has experience working in finance, most recently working in real estate.

Quality, integrity, communication

Our 3 core values. Quality work we are proud of, integrity and honesty throughout, and communication every step of the way.
